Frequently
asked questions

Answers to the most frequently asked questions.

Every counseling session will certainly be different. However, you can expect to achieve a calmer mood, clearer thoughts, and/or a mapping of problems along with action planning to thoroughly resolve the issues you are facing at their roots.

Counseling is a collaborative process between you and a psychologist. The counseling space is a safe space for you to talk about anything that is weighing on you. The psychologist will listen attentively and give you room to express yourself freely. After that, you can work together to map out the issues and create a plan to resolve those problems thoroughly, getting to the root of the issues, with the support of the psychologist.

Don't worry, everything you share in the counseling room is guaranteed to be 100% confidential by the Code of Ethics for Psychology.

No. Only a psychiatrist can prescribe medication, and our psychologist will only refer you to meet with a psychiatrist if all psychological approaches have been tried and it is found that medication is needed for your psychological recovery process. However, medication should not be the first option, due to the various risks of side effects that can arise from medication and also the various issues that cannot actually be resolved solely through medication intervention. In some cases, collaboration between psychologists and psychiatrists is needed for optimal treatment.

After counseling, the psychologist will certainlyprovide points for reflection or homework that can be done before the next session. But it's not mandatory, because counseling is a psychological recovery process that often occurs in the background without us realizing it. Whats important is the commitment to continue the journey until recovery, and the close cooperation between you and the accompanying psychologist.

You can regularly engage in good habits that support optimal mental health, such as journaling, meditation, breathing exercises, and more. The first three activities mentioned are also available for free on the Bicarakan.id app, you know.
